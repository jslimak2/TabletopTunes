How TabletopTunes Works

1. Game Analysis

When you enter a board game name, our algorithm analyzes the game's theme, mechanics, and atmosphere to understand its core characteristics.

2. Theme Matching

We use keyword detection and semantic analysis to match game themes with appropriate movie soundtrack categories:

  • Fantasy games → Epic fantasy soundtracks (Lord of the Rings, Game of Thrones)
  • Horror games → Suspenseful horror soundtracks (The Conjuring, Halloween)
  • Sci-fi games → Futuristic soundtracks (Blade Runner, Interstellar)
  • Adventure games → Heroic adventure soundtracks (Indiana Jones, Pirates of the Caribbean)

3. Soundtrack Curation

Our database contains carefully curated movie soundtracks categorized by mood, tempo, and thematic elements. Each soundtrack is hand-picked for its ability to enhance specific gaming experiences.

4. Dynamic Recommendations

The system provides real-time explanations for why each soundtrack matches your game, helping you understand the connection and choose the perfect atmospheric music.
